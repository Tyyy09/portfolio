About

Computer programming student working across software engineering & design, from Phnom Penh, now based in Barrie, Ontario.

I'm Ichty Te, a Computer Programming student with a dual focus on software engineering and design. I don't just write code or create visuals. I think about the whole product: how it functions, how it feels, and why it matters.

From firmware and machine learning at a Honda hackathon to brand identity and UI, I work across the full stack of building digital products. I care about the details most skip: spacing, hierarchy, performance, and the small interactions that make something feel finished.
